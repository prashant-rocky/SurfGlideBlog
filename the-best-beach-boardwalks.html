The Best Beach Boardwalks You Must Visit

Beach boardwalks are a classic part of American coastal travel. From the lively Santa Monica Pier to the nostalgic charm of Coney Island, these vibrant destinations offer food, games, shopping, ocean views, and endless entertainment. Whether you're traveling solo, with friends, or with family, boardwalks provide a perfect mix of fun and relaxation. This guide highlights some of the most iconic boardwalks in the U.S. and what makes each one unforgettable.

Santa Monica Pier: The West Coast Icon

Santa Monica Pier in California is one of the most famous boardwalks in the world. Packed with exciting attractions, it features an amusement park, arcade games, a historic carousel, and numerous food stalls. Visitors can enjoy oceanfront dining, take photos at the iconic Route 66 sign, or simply relax while watching the waves from the pier. With performers, shops, and lively crowds, Santa Monica Pier offers an energetic and unforgettable boardwalk experience.

Coney Island: Classic American Fun

Coney Island in New York brings a nostalgic charm that reminds visitors of traditional seaside amusement parks. Famous for its wooden boardwalk, Nathan’s Hot Dogs, and the historic Cyclone roller coaster, it’s a destination filled with memories and excitement. Families can enjoy rides, play games, visit the aquarium, or take a stroll along the beach. The mix of history, entertainment, and seaside fun makes Coney Island a timeless favorite.

Virginia Beach Boardwalk: Scenic and Relaxing

Spanning three miles along the Atlantic coast, the Virginia Beach Boardwalk offers a peaceful yet lively beach experience. Its wide path is perfect for walking, jogging, or biking. Along the way, visitors will find oceanfront restaurants, outdoor musical stages, and beautiful sculptures like the iconic King Neptune statue. The gentle ocean breeze and scenic views make it an ideal destination for families and couples.

Ocean City Boardwalk: A Family Favorite

Ocean City, Maryland is known for its family-friendly atmosphere and three-mile-long boardwalk filled with attractions. Candy shops, arcades, souvenir stores, and local eateries create a warm, welcoming environment for visitors of all ages. The soft sandy beach, combined with the boardwalk’s amusement rides and nightly entertainment, makes Ocean City one of the most enjoyable and memorable beach destinations on the East Coast.

Myrtle Beach Boardwalk: Entertainment for Everyone

Myrtle Beach offers a modern boardwalk filled with excitement. Its SkyWheel, one of the tallest Ferris wheels in the United States, provides stunning ocean views, especially at night when it lights up in vibrant colors. Visitors can explore seafood restaurants, shops, live music events, and thrill rides. With its bright, lively atmosphere, Myrtle Beach is perfect for travelers looking for fun day and night.

Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk: Historic and Fun

Located in California, Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk is one of the oldest amusement parks on the West Coast. Its iconic Giant Dipper roller coaster, arcade games, and beachside attractions make it a must-visit spot. The boardwalk sits directly on the sand, giving visitors the perfect mix of rides, food, and ocean scenery. Its historic charm and fun-filled activities make Santa Cruz a timeless coastal getaway.

What Makes Boardwalks So Special?

Boardwalks combine the beauty of the beach with the excitement of entertainment. Visitors can enjoy stunning ocean views, fresh seafood, carnival-style treats, and activities ranging from simple strolls to thrilling amusement rides. Whether you’re looking for romantic sunsets, family adventures, or vibrant nightlife, boardwalks offer something for everyone in a single, lively location.

Final Thoughts

America’s beach boardwalks offer a mix of excitement, nostalgia, and relaxation that you won’t find anywhere else. From the iconic Santa Monica Pier to the classic charm of Coney Island, each boardwalk has its own personality and attractions. Whether you’re chasing thrills, enjoying great food, or simply taking in ocean views, these destinations guarantee an unforgettable experience. Add them to your travel list—you won’t want to miss the magic of these iconic seaside spots!
